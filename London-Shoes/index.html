London Shoes

Shoes can tell a lot about a person and who they are. The first thing I notice about a person is what shoes they are wearing. We use shoes to travel places; they symbolize where we have gone, what we have done in our past, and where we will go in the future. There is a famous saying, "Before you judge, walk a mile in my shoes." You don't know what people have gone through or where they are going, but your shoes can tell that story.

About

Graphic Designer at Vector Media

Hello!

I am currently employed as a Junior Graphic Designer for Vector Media and am always open to freelance work. I graduated from the University of Connecticut with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree with a concentration in Graphic Design. I enjoyed my studies at this university, utilizing the school's resources to the best of my abilities. I have studied abroad at the University of Glasgow, Glasgow School of Art, and University of the Arts London: Central Saint Martins. Through these experiences, I was able to fully develop my skills and explore many design media platforms, including CNC plasma and 4D cinema.

Wettemann Farm

Brand Identity

The Wettemann Farm rebrand was a freelance project I worked on recently. The Wettemann Farm has been in Guilford, Connecticut, since 1907, and it was a privilege to work with them on a rebranding project.

The brand identity went through several iterations to get to where it is today. The typeface I used was Colus Regular. This typeface was the best fit for the Wettemann farm because of the classic bold letter and serifs. From my research, this is a common theme throughout farm branding. My client also wanted imagery in the brand, so I drew simple designs related to the farm. The barn in the final brand is on the Wettemann Farm property.
